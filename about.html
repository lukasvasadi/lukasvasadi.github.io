About Blog

My name is Lukas

I grew up in a relatively small town outside of New York. After college, I moved out to the UK for graduate school, whre I took the opportunity to explore Cambridgeshire, London, parts of Scotland and Wales, and even jumped across to Ireland. One time, my friend and I decided to cycle from Canterbury to the Black Forest in Germany, cutting through France, Belgium, and Luxembourg in the process. In this blog, I hope to share stories of the interesting places and people I've met along the way.
